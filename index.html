ncPick. Environmental Data. Simplified.

ncPick is a free software solution for intuitive, fast and simple data extraction and analysis from Copernicus Climate Data Store (CDS) NetCDF data files.

Why ncPick?

ncPick makes CDS NetCDF data accessible to everyone—from students to scientists—without the hassle of complex file structures.

FAQ

What file formats are supported? ▾
Windows version 1.0 accepts NetCDF files obtained through the CDS.
Does it run offline? ▾
Yes, ncPick operates entirely offline, designed to render the world map of localities without connecting to any external service and to manage all data storage locally.
How do I cite ncPick? ▾
You can cite the paper published in Data:

Jevremović, S., Arnaut, F., Kolarski, A., & Sreckovic, V. (2025). ncPick: A Lightweight Toolkit for Extracting, Analyzing, and Visualizing ECMWF ERA5 NetCDF Data Data, 10 (11), https://doi.org/10.3390/data10110178
Who are the developers? ▾
Researchers from the Institute of physics Belgrade, University of Belgrade, Republic of Serbia. The team members are Filip Arnaut, Sreten Jevremović, Aleksandra Kolarski and Vladimir A. Srećković.
